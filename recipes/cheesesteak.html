Kanas Style Philly Cheese Steak

Indulge in the bold and savory flavors of the Kansas City Style Philly Cheese Steak. This recipe features thinly sliced beef, caramelized onions, and melted cheese, all served on a soft hoagie roll. What sets this dish apart is the addition of Kansas City barbecue sauce, which infuses the meat with a smoky and tangy flavor that perfectly complements the richness of the cheese. Whether you're hosting a game day gathering or simply craving a hearty meal, the Kansas City Style Philly Cheese Steak is sure to satisfy your taste buds with its irresistible combination of savory ingredients and barbecue flair.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ pounds beef chuck roast (should be very well-marbled)
  • 3 tablespoons barbecue dry rub
  • ½ cup Kansas City style BBQ sauce
  • 4 sandwich or hoagie rolls
  • 1 (8 ounce) container cheese whiz, or sliced cheese as needed
  • ⅔ cup sautéed onions
  • ⅔ cup chopped jarred pickled peppers
  • 2 tablespoons sliced green onions

Directions

  • Preheat your grill with a griddle pan or flat top griddle to medium-high heat, aiming for a temperature range of 350-400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • While the grill is heating up, melt Cheez-Whiz in a small pot on a side burner or stovetop. Alternatively, you can melt it in the microwave following the package directions. Keep it warm until ready to use.
  • Once the griddle is hot, add butter and onions. Season the onions generously with Brisket Rub and cook them until they're softened and lightly charred, which should take around 3-5 minutes.
  • While the onions are cooking, toast your sub rolls cut side down on the griddle until they're nicely toasted.
  • Move the cooked onions to the side of the griddle and add the burnt ends. Chop and break them up on the griddle and sear for a minute or two before mixing them in with the onions. If you need directions on making burnt ends, let me know!
  • Apply a layer of the melted Cheez-Whiz to the toasted roll.
  • Load up the roll with the beef and onions mixture, then add some more Cheez-Whiz on top.
  • Grab a stack of napkins because it's time to enjoy your delicious grilled cheesesteak sandwich!
